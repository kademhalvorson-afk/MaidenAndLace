Our Story

About Maiden and Lace

Owner & Maker — Brooke Martin

I've always found joy in creating with my hands.

Before Maiden and Lace, I spent years as a wedding florist — designing romantic, nature-inspired arrangements for life's most meaningful celebrations. Over time, I began craving something slower and more lasting.

What began as a few handmade pieces for our own home slowly grew into Maiden & Lace — a collection of custom window treatments, soft home goods, and slow-made treasures inspired by motherhood, craftsmanship, and the art of home.

Each piece is made with care and intention — to bring warmth, texture, and quiet beauty to the spaces where life unfolds.

At Maiden and Lace, I believe in:

  • The beauty of handmade details
  • Creating pieces that outlast trends
  • Living slowly and surrounding yourself with things that tell a story
  • Crafting a home that feels layered, lived-in, and loved

The Process

From the first sketch to the final stitch, every piece is made with a thoughtful, hands-on approach. I work with natural fabrics, timeless pleats, and classic silhouettes — made to complement the way you live and the home you love.

Whether you're seeking custom curtains for your home or bespoke drapery for a design project, my goal is to make each step feel personal, intentional, and beautifully crafted.

When I'm not in the studio, you'll find me at home with my husband, two kids, and two dogs — spending time in the garden, sewing or knitting something new, or finding inspiration in the rhythm of everyday life. The scent of morning coffee weaving through the house. Morning light through linen curtains. The small, meaningful moments that shape a home.

— Brooke Martin
